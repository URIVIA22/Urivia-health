Common questions

Is this a medical device?

No. Urivia is a personal screening and awareness tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care or laboratory testing.

Does the app upload my data?

No. Everything stays on your phone. No account is required and nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How accurate is the color analysis?

The camera engine uses HSV color matching optimised for urine samples. For uncertain results, it automatically verifies using AI vision. Accuracy is best in natural light using a cup rather than scanning directly in the toilet.

Who is this app for?

Primarily people managing diabetes, CKD, hypertension, or prediabetes who want to monitor between doctor visits. It also works well for general hydration tracking.
