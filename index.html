What Urivia does not do

We think it's important you know exactly what this tool can and cannot do.

✕
Diagnose disease. Color alone cannot confirm or rule out any medical condition. Always follow up with a clinician for anything concerning.
✕
Replace urine lab tests. Clinical urinalysis detects protein, glucose, bacteria, and microscopic markers that no phone camera can read. Urivia screens color only.

Replace your clinician's judgment. Urivia helps you notice patterns and prepare better questions — it doesn't make clinical decisions and never will.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to create an account?
No account required — ever. Urivia stores all your scan data locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded to any server. You can use the full app anonymously from the first scan.
How accurate is photo-based color scanning?
Accuracy depends on lighting. In natural diffuse light, Urivia's calibration engine corrects for white balance and lighting to give reliable color classification. The app shows a quality score (0–100) for every scan and flags photos that are too dark, overexposed, or low-contrast. For critical concerns, always confirm with a clinical test.
Can it detect blood in my urine?
Visible blood (gross hematuria) causes a pink or red color that Urivia can flag as "Seek care." However, microscopic hematuria — too small to change color — requires a clinical urine dipstick or lab test. If you have any concern about blood in your urine, see a doctor regardless of what Urivia shows.
Is my health data private?
Yes. All scan data — colors, history, symptoms, results — is stored exclusively on your device. No images are uploaded. No scan results leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to export a report. Urivia has no server-side database of your health data.
My urine is bright yellow after vitamins — is that a problem?
Completely harmless. Bright neon yellow after B-vitamins (especially riboflavin/B2) is a normal effect of water-soluble vitamin excretion. Urivia recognizes this color and will note it as supplement-related when you indicate vitamins in the symptom check.
Does the Pro plan auto-renew? Can I cancel?
Pro subscriptions renew monthly or annually through the App Store or Google Play. You can cancel at any time in your device's subscription settings — no cancellation fees. If you cancel, Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.
