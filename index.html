Common questions

Is this a medical device?

No. UriVia Health is a personal screening and awareness tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical care or laboratory testing.

Does the app upload my data?

No. Everything stays on your phone. No account is required and nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How accurate is the color analysis?

The camera engine uses HSV color matching optimised for urine samples. For uncertain results it automatically verifies using AI vision. Best results in natural light using a cup.

Who is this for?

Primarily people managing diabetes, CKD, hypertension, or prediabetes who want to monitor between doctor visits. Also great for general hydration tracking.

Can I use it without a subscription?

Yes. The free plan includes 10 lifetime scans with no account required. Pro unlocks unlimited scans, full history, AI advisor, and PDF reports.
